Found Someone's Item?

Thank you for your honesty! Follow these instructions to help return the item to its owner.

Submission Process

Important Information

All found items should be submitted to the BAO Office. The staff there will handle documenting and storing the item until it can be returned to its owner.

1

Bring the Item to the BAO Office

As soon as possible, please bring the found item to the BAO Office located in the Student Center building.

Office Hours:

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Closed on weekends and holidays

2

Provide Information About the Item

You'll be asked to share some details about where and when you found the item. This information helps us:

  • Contact the rightful owner more quickly
  • Verify the item belongs to someone claiming it
  • Maintain accurate records of all found items
3

Receive Documentation (Optional)

If you'd like, you can request a receipt confirming that you turned in a found item. This can be useful if:

  • The owner wants to thank you personally
  • You want to follow up on whether the item was claimed

Helpful Guidelines

Act Promptly

Please submit found items as soon as possible. Someone is likely actively searching for their lost possession.

Handle with Care

Avoid opening bags, wallets, or accessing personal information. Simply turn in items as you found them.

For Valuable Items

If you find a particularly valuable item (electronic device, wallet, jewelry), please deliver it to the office as soon as possible.

Check for ID Tags

If the item has a QR code or other identification tag, you may be able to contact the owner directly, but we still recommend bringing the item to the BAO Office.

BAO Office Details

Location & Contact

  • Student Center Building, 1st Floor, Room 103
  • (123) 456-7890
  • lostfound@campus.edu

Thank You!

Your honesty and thoughtfulness in turning in found items helps maintain a supportive community. Many students are able to recover their valuable possessions thanks to people like you.
